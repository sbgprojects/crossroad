We Meet the Expectations and Needs of Our Clients

We are an established group in Qatar that specialises in the supply of contract manpower. Established in 2014 and having more than 1500 employees in Qatar, we are counted among the top service providers in the market. We focus on increasing productivity through an excellent quality of work, efficiency and knowledge, this enables client to concentrate on his core business objectives while Cross Road manages resources through very dedicated staff.

Our aim is to add value to client’s business operations through the provision of skilled, experienced and proficient manpower. We ensure our clients obtain quality personnel in specialized categories through a stringent selection and deployment process using our significant connectivity to the manpower market. With over ten years’ experience in our basket we know how to get the job done.

Our system allows us to respond quickly to our clients’ request drawing from a pool of tested resources with the experience and skills needed. When speed of deployment is a primary consideration, Cross Road should be first to approach. We are passionate about supporting our customers and always here to serve them with honesty, dedication and sincerity. We provide skilled and unskilled work force of all nationality on short- and long-term contract to align with your requirements and this allows clients to deploy his workforce to meet specific needs. Our presence on the ground and our proven track record puts Cross Road in a perfect position to respond immediately and effectively to our clients’ needs. Our professionalism, dedication, honest dealing, timely payment to workers has put us in most wanted workforce in environment.
