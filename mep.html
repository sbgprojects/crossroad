Our Services

MEP Services

We are one of the Upcoming Electro-Mechanical & Engineering Company in the Qatar, undertaking Mechanical, Plumbing, Fire Fighting, Fire Alarm, Electrical and Low Current System Projects. Our principal activities include, installation, testing, commissioning and maintenance of works.

We have the expertise to offer a full range of electro-mechanical services as follows:

Plumbing Works

Plumbing work includes Storage Tanks, Transfer pumps, Water Heaters, Water Filters, Cold Water System and Hot Water System using Copper/PPR/CPVC Distribution.

Drainage Works

Drainage Work includes UPVC/ESVC/DI Soil, Waste, Vent Rain water pipes, Storm Sewage, lifting station, Floor Drains, Floor Clean outs, Floor gullies, Road Gullies, Gully traps, Overflows, Bottle Traps, Manholes, Sump pits, Pumps, Holding tank and final connection to the Municipal Drainage and Disposal system in the vicinity.

Electrical Works

Electrical Works includes HV Substation, Unit Substations, Package Substations, Transformers, 50Hz, 400Hz, 60Hz Frequency Converters, 400Hz Service points, HV/LV Cables, Main, Sub Main & Final Distribution Panels, Small Power, Lighting, Dimming/Lighting Control, Central Battery, Generators, Synchronising Panels, ATS/MTS, UPS, Grounding & Lighting Protection system.

Low Current Systems

LC/ELV System includes Telephone, Data, Access Control System, CCTV System, PA/BGM System, SMATV System, Open Conference System, Video Conference System, Audio Visual System, Audio Door Entry System, Audio/Video Door Entry System, Intruder Alarm System, Road Barrier System, Car Parking Management System, Servant Call System, and Structured Cabling with networking server.
