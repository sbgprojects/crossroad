Our Services

Civil Works

Started with a vision of becoming a full-fledged Construction and maintenance service provider, we specialise in Management, Coordination and delivery of diversified services across construction and maintenance sectors

Supported by strong pool of skilled workforce, excellent labor relationship and able leadership, in a short duration of our operations, we are trusted by contractors of major projects in Qatar to work as their trusted subcontractor as well as by established foreign companies.

Construction
  • Warehouse construction and Steel Structure works.
  • Dry wall partitions and Gypsum Ceiling works.
  • Paint works for residential and commercial projects.
  • Hard landscaping works like laying paver blocks, curb stones etc.
  • Masonry works like tiles, block, plaster, screed etc.
  • Metal works like handrails, gratings, gates, grills, ladders, fence, etc.
Maintenance
  • Renovation and modification work.
  • Maintenance and upgrading works including sanitary, plumbing and carpentry.
  • Repainting, replacement of tiles, marbles, floorings, brick work and plaster repair etc.
  • Corrective maintenance including that of steel structure, roofing/cladding and paint works.
  • Routine and yearly/multiyear maintenance contracts.
  • Metal works like handrails, gratings, gates, grills, ladders, fence, etc.
